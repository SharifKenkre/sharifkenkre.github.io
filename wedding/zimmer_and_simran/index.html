Grand Mercure

The Grand Mercure in Vodlemol Cacora, Goa, is a hotel known for its beautiful settings, which often makes it a popular choice for weddings and special events. It likely offers a lovely atmosphere, comfortable accommodations, and various facilities that would be perfect for Zimmer and Simran's big day, ensuring a memorable celebration in a picturesque location.

FAQ

What is the dress code?

We kindly request that guests adhere to a formal dress code for our wedding. We encourage you to choose an outfit that makes you feel comfortable, while maintaining the formal tone of the occasion.

Can I bring a plus one?

Yes, you are welcome to bring a plus one if this is specified in your RSVP. If you don't see the option but would like to bring a partner or significant other, please let us know and we will try our best to accommodate them.

Is it a child friendly event?

Yes, it is a child-friendly event. You can easily RSVP for your child when responding for yourself.

Will there be a children's meal?

Yes, there will be! If you RSVP "yes" for your child, we will be in touch closer to the date to confirm the children's menu with you.
