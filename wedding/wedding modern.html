How We Met

Savia and Ian first crossed paths at a mutual friend's birthday party in the summer of 2019. What started as a casual conversation about their shared love for hiking and photography quickly blossomed into something beautiful.

After countless adventures, late-night talks, and supporting each other through life's ups and downs, Ian proposed during a sunset hike at their favorite trail - the same place where they had their first date.
