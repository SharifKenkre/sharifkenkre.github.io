Hengrave Hall

Located just outside the charming town of Bury St Edmunds, Hengrave Hall is a stunning and historic venue that beautifully blends elegance and character. Set within picturesque grounds, this 16th century Tudor manor house offers a romantic atmosphere—perfect for a wedding. The moment we laid eyes on it, we knew it was the place where we would be married.

Directions

For our wedding guests travelling from abroad:

From the United States & India:

We recommend flying into either London Heathrow Airport or London Gatwick Airport. Bury St Edmunds is approximately a two hour journey from either airport. You can take a train from London, or if you prefer, we would be happy to help arrange a taxi for a smooth and comfortable journey.

Additionally, we will be arranging a private coach from London Heathrow to Bury St Edmunds on the morning of Tuesday the 9th of September. If you are arriving any time before 12 noon, or would like to coordinate your flights to join the coach, please let us know in advance so we can include you.

From Europe:

We suggest flying into London Stansted Airport, which is only about a 45 minute drive to Bury St Edmunds. It is the most convenient option for our European guests.

For Local Guests:

We have included a small map of Hengrave with the invitation to help guide you to the main entrance of the venue. If you require further directions or travel advice, please do not hesitate to get in touch.

FAQ

What is the dress code?

We kindly request that guests adhere to a formal dress code for our wedding. We encourage you to choose an outfit that makes you feel comfortable, while maintaining the formal tone of the occasion.

Can I bring a plus one?

Yes, you are welcome to bring a plus one if this is specified in your RSVP. If you don't see the option but would like to bring a partner or significant other, please let us know and we will try our best to accommodate them.

Is it a child friendly event?

Yes, it is a child-friendly event. You can easily RSVP for your child when responding for yourself.

Will there be a children's meal?

Yes, there will be! If you RSVP "yes" for your child, we will be in touch closer to the date to confirm the children's menu with you.
